History

Jimeta Division, located in Jimeta, Adamawa State, Nigeria, is a key part of the Nigerian Police Force. Over the years, this division has established itself as a community-driven, integrity-based police department. With its roots deep in the history of Nigeria, Jimeta Division has focused on providing safety and security for residents and ensuring law and order. This division has seen tremendous growth and has evolved with the times, adjusting its approach to policing with modern technologies and methods. The division plays a vital role in maintaining peace and order while upholding the rights of citizens. Its commitment to justice and public service has earned it recognition as one of the most reliable divisions in the region.
